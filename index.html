Victoria's Road Crashes in 2018

What month and day has the most car accidents?

The road crashes in Victoria are visualised by each month in 2018. It is broken down by two factors, which is whether they occurred in Metro or Country Victoria. It is evident that Metro Victoria experienced a greater amount of road crashes, where this could be attributed to the greater population and settlement in Metro Victoria compared to Country Victoria. In 2018, March (1,027) experienced the highest number of road crashes, followed by May (922). On the contrary, September experienced the least number of road crashes (761).
